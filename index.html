Welcome to the Open Source Studio Website!

Our website and official documents are still under construction! Stay tuned!

We are currently a national organization, with a mission to not only teach coding virtually, but to also allow students to contribute to large-scale open-source coding projects!

The CS fields we are teaching include Python and Web Development, Game Development with Unity, and Discord Bot Development (w/ Discord.js).

We accept members regardless of school, experience, or location. All you have to do is join our Discord!

P.S... we're hiring! Department apps can be found here. We also have Leadership applications open here.
