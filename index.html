We are currently a national organization, with a mission to not only teach coding virtually, but to also help students create large-scale coding projects on GitHub!

The CS fields we are teaching include Python and Web Development, Game Development with Unity, and Discord.js Bot Development.

We accept members regardless of school, experience, or location. All you have to do is join our Discord!
